Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

German Exam Simulator ("the App") is developed by GermanLang ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • Voice Recordings: When you use the speaking exam feature, your voice is temporarily recorded and sent to a secure server for speech-to-text conversion. Recordings are processed in real-time and are NOT stored permanently.
  • Exam Answers: Your written answers and exam responses are sent to a secure server for AI-powered evaluation. These are processed in real-time and are NOT stored permanently.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to premium features, purchase data is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To convert your speech to text during speaking exams
  • To evaluate your exam answers using AI
  • To generate AI voice responses during speaking practice
  • To restore your purchases across devices

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For exam content delivery and secure API routing. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For speech recognition, AI conversation, text-to-speech, and exam evaluation. Your data is processed through our secure server and is not used by OpenAI for training. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • We do NOT create user accounts or require login.
  • We do NOT store your voice recordings, exam answers, or personal data on our servers.
  • All data processing happens in real-time and is discarded after the response is generated.
  • Exam content is downloaded from secure servers (Supabase) over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • API communications are routed through secure server-side functions. No API keys are stored in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for language learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us.

7. Microphone Permission

The App requests microphone access ONLY when you enter the speaking exam section. This is required for the speech recognition feature. You can deny this permission, but the speaking exam will not function without it. Your audio is processed in real-time and is not stored.

8. Internet Connection

The App requires an active internet connection to function. Exam content, AI evaluation, and speech features all require server communication.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
